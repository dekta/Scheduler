Meet Helena Eitel

Helena graduated from Dartmouth College with a B.A. in biology and studio art. She was born and raised in Seattle, where she spent many great days swimming in Lake Washington with her extended family members close by. Helena just moved to San Francisco and is currently working as a freelance graphic designer for the World Bank Group and other clients.

Helena has always loved school, and she is excited to be tutoring a wide variety of subjects including chemistry, biology, english, and French. In high school she learned that she benefit a great deal from wonderful mentors in her life and from being a mentor for her younger brother and cousins. She was also an informal mentor to many underclassmen as captain of the coed tennis team.
